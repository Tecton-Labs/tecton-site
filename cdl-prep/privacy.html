Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Tecton Labs ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in the Tecton Labs CDL Prep 2026 app.

2. Information Collection

  • Personal Data: We do not require account registration. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Study Progress: Your quiz results, "Readiness Score," and unique question IDs are stored locally on your device to track your mastery.
  • Payment Data: All transactions are handled by Apple. We do not store financial information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Apple App Store for payments and RevenueCat to manage your Pro Lifetime Access via anonymous identifiers.

4. Data Retention

Study data stays on your device. Deleting the app removes local progress. Pro status is linked to your Apple ID and can be recovered via the "Restore Purchase" button.
