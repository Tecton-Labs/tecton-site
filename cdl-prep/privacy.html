Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

Tecton Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CDL Prep 2026 mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Our Commitment: We believe in privacy by design. CDL Prep 2026 is designed to work primarily offline, and we collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Quiz progress and scores
  • Study preferences and settings
  • Exam date (if you choose to set one)
  • Category selections for your license goals

This information is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information Collected Through Third-Party Services:

  • RevenueCat: If you make in-app purchases, RevenueCat processes payment information. We do not have access to your payment card details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: Apple may collect analytics about app usage. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

The locally stored information is used to:

  • Track your study progress
  • Calculate your readiness score
  • Provide personalized study recommendations
  • Remember your preferences

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your study data remains on your device.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the app
  • Use the "Reset Progress" feature in settings
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings

Children's Privacy

CDL Prep 2026 is intended for users who are preparing for their Commercial Driver's License exam, typically adults 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
