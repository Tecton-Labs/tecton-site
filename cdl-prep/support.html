Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Your Pro status is linked to your Apple ID. Simply download the app on your new device, and your purchase should restore automatically. If not, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases."
How do I reset my progress?
Go to Settings in the app and scroll down to find "Reset Progress." Please note this action cannot be undone and will clear all your study data.
What is the Readiness Score?
Your Readiness Score shows how prepared you are based on your performance across all the endorsements you've selected. Aim for 95% or higher before taking your official exam to qualify for our Pass Guarantee.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! All 750+ questions and study materials are stored locally on your device. You can study anywhere, even without an internet connection.
How do I claim the 95% Pass Guarantee refund?
If you achieved a 95% Readiness Score before your exam but didn't pass, email a copy of your official failure notice to tectonlabs.ca@gmail.com within 30 days of your exam.
How do I request a refund?
Standard refunds are processed through the Apple App Store. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for your purchase.
